Colombia GDP



Evolution of the GDP and the agriculture participation in this in Colombia between 2006 and 2017.



Insights

  • In general, when agriculture participation on the GDP increases, the total GDP increases. But some years, like 2014 and 2015, the opposite happens.
  • The growth of the GDP doesn't follow the drift that it's normally expected in every economy.
  • It can be seen that Colombia's economy it's being affected by bigger economies (e.g. US economy and it's financial crisis of 2007–2008).

Data abstraction

What

  • The dataset type is Table.

Attributes

  • Year: Temporal
  • Total GDP percentage growth: Ordered - Quantitative - Diverging
  • Percentage growth of the agriculture participation in the GDP: Ordered - Quantitative - Diverging

Why

  • The objective of this viz is show the growth of the GDP and the participation of the agriculture in this. Discover - Trends
  • Identify some extrange behaviour through the years. Identify - Outliers
  • Identify any relation between GDP and the participation of the agriculture in this. Discover - Correlation
